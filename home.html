About Me

Hello welcome to my porfolio, I'm Mario De La Torre Ochoa, I've studied software development and web development, preferring more a frontend work. All my life I've been working with computers since I was a kid, I started playing games at a young age, since then, I always wanted to work as a game designer, I even made my own little games which only my friends and I played. After a while, I changed my mind, and wanted to be a designer, but also wanted to program, so I choose web development focusing on frontend so I could actually do both things at once.
